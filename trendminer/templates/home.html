About TrendMiner

The goal of the TrendMiner project is to deliver innovative, portable open-source real-time methods for cross-lingual mining and summarisation of large-scale stream media, focusing on two use cases in the fields of sentiments detection in finance and politics. TrendMiner implements for this an inter-disciplinary approach, combining deep linguistic methods from text processing, knowledge-based reasoning from web science, machine learning, economics, and political science.
